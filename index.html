I am a physics PhD candidate at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. My research focuses on ultracold atoms, optical lattices, quantum simulation, and transport in disordered many-body systems.

Research

I work on diffusion dynamics in optical lattice systems, with an emphasis on transport in Bose-Hubbard and related many-body models.
